I am a mechanical engineer studying at Olin College of Engineering located outside Boston. I have a passion for building things and I am captivated by the design, fabrication, testing, and prototyping workflow. In addition to my wide assortment of projects, I have an ever-growing repertoire of manufacturing skills that I employ daily to bring my ideas to life.

In my free time, I enjoy building and flying FPV drones as well as producing cinematic videos from the flight footage.

I hope that my portfolio will help to demonstrate my attention to detail as well as the quality and variety of my work. Enjoy!
